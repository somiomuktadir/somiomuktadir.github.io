AI for Governance - 07.08.2025

AI has the power to change our lives for good and contribute to the governance of the humankind. Apart from the fact that overuse and misuse of AI raise security concerns and may result in lacking creativity and unused (wasted) analytical power; it has the power to change the very structure of the modern world in an irreversible way. AI is fundamentally restructuring our daily lives and is on the way to changing the economic structure of the world. Education, research, health, military, business efficiency : AI is everywhere. But have you ever thought what would happen if AI takes over the government one day?

Little Encouragements - 13.09.2025

What changes the course of life? For me, it has always been little words of encouragements and a small boost-up of confidence. I have learnt the hard way that controlling emotions, channeling them in productive paths and being focused on what one does with a clear plan always brings up positive results. To err is human and I often fumble and fail to control emotions. But things have been going nice in the recent days. Pressure of work and constantly brewing new ideas are what keep me distracted, in a positive way.
